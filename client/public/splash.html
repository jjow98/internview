What is InternView?

A quick summary of what InternView offers:

Search through Years of Internship Data

InternView contains 5+ years of data from students across all disciplines and corporate, research, and Princeton-specific internship opportunities.

Prepare with Past Interview Questions

InternView gives you access to what Princeton students have to say about the questions you'll face during internship interviews and how you can best prepare for them through courses, books, and more!

Learn About Internship Experiences

InternView provides honest reviews about internship experiences by Princeton students and for Princeton students.
